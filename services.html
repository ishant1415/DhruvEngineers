Our Services

Comprehensive Industrial Solutions

Each project is handled with meticulous attention to detail, ensuring that we meet or exceed your specifications with the best quality.

Machine Shop Services

Precision Machining Excellence

Our advanced machine shop is equipped with state-of-the-art CNC machines, VMCs, and conventional equipment to handle a wide range of precision machining requirements. All types of machine shop work are executed with precision and excellence.

  • Turning Operations: Precision turning for cylindrical parts with tight tolerances
  • Milling Services: Complex contour milling, gear cutting, and keyway slotting
  • Drilling & Tapping: Precise hole creation and threading in various materials
  • CNC Lathe Jobs: Automated precision for complex, repeatable parts
  • VMC Precision Work: Multi-axis machining for complex geometries
  • Boring Operations: Precision enlargement of holes with our horizontal boring machine

We work with a variety of materials including stainless steel, mild steel, aluminum, brass, and specialized alloys to meet your specific requirements.

Fabrication Services

Custom Metal Fabrication

Our fabrication team specializes in creating custom metal products for various industries, with particular expertise in food processing, pharmaceutical, and industrial equipment. We handle all types of SS fabrication products customized to customer requirements, as well as mild steel fabrication.

  • Stainless Steel Tanks: Custom SS tanks in grades 310, 304, and 316 as per specific requirements
  • Industrial Chimneys: Durable, code-compliant chimney systems
  • Canteen Equipment: Tables, trays, and other food service equipment
  • Industrial Hoppers: Material handling solutions for various applications
  • Conveyors & Trolleys: Custom transport and material handling solutions
  • Cabinets & Furniture: Custom SS cabinets, chairs, and other furniture
  • MS Brackets & Frames: Support systems and structural frameworks designed for specific needs

We are known for providing the best quality in all our fabrication work, with thorough quality checks throughout the production process. Our expertise extends to both stainless steel and mild steel fabrication for a wide range of industrial applications.
